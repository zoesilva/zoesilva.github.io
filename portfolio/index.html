CREATIVE CODING

I'm Zoe Silva, a New Media & Digital Design student at Fordham University, with a concentration in Art, Text, and Design. My journey as a designer began with a deep passion for print design, where I honed my skills in creating visually compelling layouts and engaging narratives through physical media. However, my creative path took an exciting turn last semester when I enrolled in an Intro to Web Design course at Fordham. This experience ignited a newfound love for web design, where I discovered the dynamic possibilities of crafting interactive and user-centered digital experiences. This website was my final project for that class.
